# Beauty System

## Overview
The Beauty Score is a passive farm rating that reflects how decorated and lively the farm looks. It doesn't affect crop yields or core gameplay — it's a soft progression layer that rewards players who invest in aesthetics.

Beauty influences NPC quest rewards and may unlock special cosmetic items in the future.

---

## Beauty Score

The farm has a numeric Beauty Score visible to the player (e.g. on the computer UI or a small sign near the house).

| Score Range | Label | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 0–99 | Plain Farm | No bonus |
| 100–299 | Tidy Farm | +5% coin reward on regular quests |
| 300–599 | Charming Farm | +10% coin reward on regular quests, better item rewards |
| 600–999 | Lovely Farm | +15% quest rewards, occasional surprise gift from Rosa |
| 1000+ | Dream Farm | +20% quest rewards, Mayor Chen offers exclusive gate quests |

---

## What Contributes to Beauty Score

### Pets
| Pet | Beauty Points |
|---|---|
| Dog | +80 |
| Cat | +60 |
| Chicken | +30 |
| Goat | +40 |

Pets are always active — they roam the farm and contribute as long as they're not stolen.

*Note: Dog triggers retribution quests more frequently. Higher beauty, higher risk.*

### Decorations
| Item | Beauty Points |
|---|---|
| Garden Statue | +50 |
| Windmill | +70 |
| Scarecrow | +30 |
| Flower Bed | +25 |
| Lantern Post | +35 |
| Painted Fence (per section) | +10 |

### Crop Variety Bonus
Planting a mix of crops across tiers gives a passive beauty boost:

| Condition | Bonus Points |
|---|---|
| 3+ different crops planted at once | +20 |
| All 3 tiers represented on the farm | +50 |
| Every crop type planted at least once (all 9) | +100 (one-time) |

---

## MANA Cosmetics (Future)
Special pets and decorations purchasable with MANA. These are purely cosmetic — they contribute beauty points like their coin-bought equivalents, but offer unique visual designs.

Examples:
- Rare breed dog (golden/spotted variant) — same beauty value as standard dog
- Seasonal decorations (harvest lanterns, winter wreaths)
- Premium fence skins

*MANA items never provide more beauty points than their coin equivalents. No pay-to-win path.*

---

## Multiplayer / Visitor Visibility
When another Decentraland player visits your farm:
- They see your farm in its current visual state (decorations, pets, crops)
- They can leave a "like" — this does not affect Beauty Score but is visible to the owner
- Beauty Score is visible on a small sign near the entrance

---

## Notes
- Beauty is additive — every item helps, nothing decrements it (except dog theft, which removes the dog's points until recovered)
- There is no "beauty decay" — once you buy it, it counts
- Future: leaderboard of most beautiful farms visible from the computer
